MORE ABOUT ME

I was born in a lovely and liberal family in Beijing, China. My parents supported me and held positive opinions about my thoughts all the time. I’m really grateful that they always encouraged me to explore the world in the way I like, so I had a very good time as well as had a lot of resources and chances to access to new things and techniques during my childhood and teenage years.

After stepping into the middle school, here came more pressure about study and life, since I was the kind of person who has exact demands on myself. To relax my intense mind at the spare time, I began to make little stuff by hand and then gave them to my friends. It began with simple forms such as cards or albums and then moved to leather, woods, and pop-up books. When I gave my presents to people around me, seeing their happy faces could always cheer me up. As I kept making stuff, in my high school years, I made some edited videos and live demos. I also designed an English learning website. My mom often told me that my works really convinced her that digital media art would be a great area for me to explore. After graduation, I succeeded in getting into the School of Animation and Digital Arts at the Communication University of China.

At the third year of college, I had fewer courses and more time of my own. I read a lot of books, traveled to different countries, and started to care more about the society and the world. Homosexuality Parade, Depression, Dystopia, Religious Difference ... The diversity and culture were impacting my thinking. Simple forms like web or app are no longer enough to meet my needs. I want to find a new form to express my opinion, awaken people's mind and no real changes to the world.

Once I occasionally stepped in an art exhibition held by Team Lab in the Forbidden City. And I was so moved by the great view and the interaction with people. After that time, I met with a lot of design works that combine new techniques. And now I believe, design with technology is the new way to achieve my goal. The more I learn about technology-based design, the more I fall in love with it. It is the combination of aesthetics and science, form and content. It can cross language, politics as well as culture. It makes people interact with it naturally, lead happy and comfortable lives or think more deeply, which is exactly what I am looking for.

If you want to know more about me, you could download my cv or contact me by email . Nice to have you.
